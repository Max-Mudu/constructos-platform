## Descriptions

### Description (4000 chars max)
```
ConstructOS is a complete construction site management platform built for field teams, supervisors, and project managers.

ATTENDANCE TRACKING
Mark worker attendance in seconds. Supervisors can bulk-check in entire crews. GPS-verified check-ins ensure accuracy. Full history and export to CSV.

LABOUR MANAGEMENT
Log daily labour entries by trade, hours, and rate. Filter by project, site, or date range. Export detailed labour cost reports for invoicing and payroll.

DELIVERY MANAGEMENT
Record material deliveries with photos, supplier details, and quantity. Track acceptance status. Never lose a delivery docket again.

REAL-TIME NOTIFICATIONS
Get instant alerts for pending approvals, new deliveries, and project updates. Push notifications keep your team in sync across sites.

ROLE-BASED ACCESS
Every user sees exactly what they need. Workers check in and view their history. Supervisors manage their site. Project managers see across all projects. Admins have full control.

REPORTS & EXPORTS
Generate attendance, labour, and delivery reports. Export to CSV or PDF. Share directly from the app.

WORKS OFFLINE
Field conditions vary. ConstructOS queues attendance and labour submissions when offline and syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

Requires an active ConstructOS company account. Contact your company administrator for access.
```
