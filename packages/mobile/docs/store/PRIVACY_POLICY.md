# ConstructOS Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

ConstructOS ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a construction site management platform operated by [Your Company Name] ("Company"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the ConstructOS mobile application.

By using ConstructOS, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Full name (first name, last name)
- Email address
- Job role and company affiliation
- Account credentials (passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext)

### 2.2 Attendance Data
- Date and time of attendance check-ins and check-outs
- Site and project association for each attendance record
- Check-in method (self, supervisor-marked)

### 2.3 Labour Records
- Daily labour hours logged per worker
- Trade type, hourly rate, and total cost per entry
- Project and site association

### 2.4 Delivery Records
- Supplier name, material description, and quantity
- Delivery date and acceptance status
- Photographs of delivery receipts or materials (only when you choose to capture them)

### 2.5 Device and Technical Data
- Push notification token (for delivering alerts to your device)
- App version and operating system (for error reporting)
- We do **not** collect precise GPS coordinates unless explicitly enabled for attendance verification by your company administrator

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

- Provide core app functionality (attendance, labour, deliveries, reports)
- Send push notifications for job-related alerts and updates
- Generate reports for project management and payroll purposes
- Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access
- Improve app performance and fix bugs

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on servers controlled by your company or its designated cloud provider
- Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) at all times
- Authentication tokens are stored in the device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
- We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data

## 6. Photos and Camera Access

The App requests camera access solely to photograph delivery receipts and site materials. Photos are uploaded to your company's server and associated with the relevant delivery record. Photos are not shared with third parties and are not used for any purpose other than the delivery record they were captured for.

---

## 7. Notifications

The App requests permission to send push notifications for work-related alerts (new deliveries, attendance reminders, project updates). You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the App.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as your company account is active and as required by applicable law. When a company account is closed, data may be retained for up to 90 days before deletion unless a shorter period is required by law.
